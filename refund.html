Refund Policy

Effective: January 1, 2026 · Last updated: April 2026

At OwnIt, client satisfaction is the priority. Every app is built custom from scratch for each client, which means significant time and effort goes into each project before delivery. This policy exists to be fair to both parties.

The 2-Week Trial Period

Every OwnIt app comes with a 2-week trial period starting from the date the live link is delivered. During this time:

1
Use It in Real Life
Log your actual expenses, explore every tab, and test the app with your real workflow. This is the best way to know if it fits your needs.
2
Request Any Changes
If anything feels off — a category name, a tab label, a color, a layout — reach out and we'll fix it. Revisions during the trial period are free of charge.
3
Confirm Satisfaction
At the end of the 2 weeks, the project is considered complete and accepted. No further free revisions will be included after this point.

Refund Eligibility

If you are not satisfied with your app after the 2-week trial period — even after free revisions — you may request a partial refund under the following conditions:

50% refund will be issued if dissatisfaction is reported within the 2-week trial window and cannot be resolved through revisions. Upon issuing the refund, the app link will be unpublished and access to the deployed application will be removed.

Refund requests must be submitted via email to Ownitapp.info@yahoo.com before the end of the 2-week trial period. Requests submitted after the trial period has ended will not be eligible for a refund.

What Is Not Refundable

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • ✗ Refund requests submitted after the 2-week trial period
  • ✗ Post-delivery revision fees ($50 aesthetic / $75 functional)
  • ✗ Add-on fees (additional businesses, Supabase multi-device sync)
  • ✗ Cases where the client has not communicated concerns during the trial

How to Request a Refund

Email Ownitapp.info@yahoo.com with the subject line "Refund Request" and include:

  • → Your name and app name
  • → The date your live link was delivered
  • → A brief description of why the app did not meet your needs

Refunds will be processed within 5–7 business days of approval.

Questions about this policy? Reach out anytime at Ownitapp.info@yahoo.com — we are always happy to talk through any concerns before, during, or after your build.
